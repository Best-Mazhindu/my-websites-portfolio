our customer SERVICES

MPS Security is a customer-oriented organization which operates with integrity and honesty. As an integral part of our operations, we have prioritized the protection of our clients’ assets and have vowed to do our best to enhance their businesses.
In a bid to strengthen our relationships with them, we go the extra mile to create a mutually beneficial and lasting relationship with our stakeholders through personalized attention. We visit our clients’ offices occasionally to establish the condition of our service as compared to the clients’ expectations and adjustments are promptly effected as per their requirements.
When it comes to complaints from our clients, we recognize that speed is of utmost importance in providing feedback and making sure solutions are quickly found and effected. We are proactive in identifying the clients’ needs and meeting them.
As a company, we always look to utilize our relationships with clients to build and increase ourmarket share.
Our dedication to the delivery of excellent services sums up our competitive edge. While the primary purpose of a business is to make a profit, MPS Security’s approach is based on the ideal that profit/loss follows the success or failure of meeting the clients’ needs, be they personal or business.

MPS is a defence, security and intelligence organization, based in Harare, Zimbabwe, whose primary objective is to deliver high-quality services in crime prevention and overall maintenance of law and order in Zimbabwe.
