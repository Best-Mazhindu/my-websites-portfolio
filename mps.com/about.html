MPS SECURITY SERVICES

MACRO PERSONNEL SERVICES t/a MPS SECURITY is a defence, security and intelligence organization, based in Harare, Zimbabwe, whose primary objective is to deliver high-quality services in crime prevention and overall maintenance of law and order in Zimbabwe.

We are a risk management solutions firm which provides comprehensive security services which enable our clients to effectively manage the consequences thereof. Our aim is to be the all-inclusive remedy for our varied clients’ long and short-term security needs. We aim to help map out and act upon the set guidelines to specific plans based on our clients’ vision by providing the most effective ways of utilizing resources from economic and financial standpoints, while maintaining quality services.

The recognition of such opportunities is one of the major motivations of the organization and we subsequently strive to create a compelling future for security services, which goes beyond the basics provided by others to date, as well as our planning horizon, a vision that will see us recognized as a provider of world-class innovative security solutions.
