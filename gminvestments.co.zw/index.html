Civil loans and Business loans

Green Masters Investments (GMI) is a Zimbabwean registered company offering consumer loans to the public. The company has been offering loans since 2007. Our loans meet the day-to-day needs which assist in maintaining the clients’ lifestyle. Unlike banks and other micro-finance institutions, there is no waiting period for receiving loans once the full requirements are met. There are no closing periods for offering loans as they are available throughout the month.

We provide flexibility of repayment whereby the borrower has three repayment options to choose from. Firstly the loan can be repaid in cash within one month. If the client fails to raise the full repayment amount, they have the second option to come and pay the interest component and have the loan rolled over to the following month. The third option is a repayment period of four months with the amount being deducted from the client’s salary through the employer Explore More

Services We Offer.

To provide micro-finance solutions by offering innovative products and services in order to meet stakeholder expectations

Personal Loan

We offer salary based loans to permanent employees of approved companies. The repayments are deducted from the individuals’ salary by the employer who then remits the money to us. The loan is instant, once all the requirements are met

Business Loan

Green Masters also offers loans to small entrepreneurs to assist in boosting their businesses. The loans are repayable over a period of one to six months. The loan is available in a week or two once all vetting procedures are complete and satisfactory

Assistance

We also offer training services on how to minimize costs and maximize profits in order to keep the business as a going concern. Advice is also offered to individuals to help keep their credit to a minimum.

Repayment options

We provide flexibility of repayment whereby the borrower has three repayment options to choose from. Firstly the loan can be repaid in cash within one month. If the client fails to raise the full repayment amount, they have the second option to come and pay the interest component and have the loan rolled over to the following month. The third option is a repayment period of four months with the amount being deducted from the client’s salary through the employer
