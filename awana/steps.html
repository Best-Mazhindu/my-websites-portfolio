How Do i apply ??

Follow The Steps Listed Below :

Step 01: Go through Requirements to Apply

Below you can find the general requirements for all courses, although some courses might have additional requirements which must be confirmed by contcating us

  • Completely filled Online Application / Offline Application form
  • Copy of International Passport
  • Copy of National ID Card
  • Copies of previous qualifications (as applicable)
  • Grade 10 or 'O' Level (for Diploma programmes)​
  • Grade 12 or 'A' Level (for Bachelor Degree programmes)
  • Bachelor's Degree Certificate & All semester transcripts (for Master Degree programmes)
  • Master's Degree Certificate & All semester transcripts (for Ph.D programmes)

*Note : Minimum 50% aggregate marks in previous qualification are required for eligibility with related subjects. Individual requirements for each course can be found on the respective course details page

Step 03 : Go through the Students Visa Application

Documents Required

Acceptance Letter / Visa Letter issued from the university after the payment of registration fee.
Original Passport with a validity of more than 6 months at the time of application, with at least 4 blank pages.
Print out of properly filled up online visa application form with uploaded picture and signatures.
Receipt of Registration fee payment, issued by Edudite with logo and signature of concerned authority.
2 recent photos of size 51 mm x 51 mm (Not older than 3 months, No uniform) with white background and DARK DRESS and frontal view. Eyebrows, Ears, Forehead and Chin to be clearly visible. Edited, Blurred or Unclear photos will not be accepted.
3 months bank statement of the Sponsor, with sufficient funds, and a copy of Sponsor's National ID and Sponsorship Letter, with address proof.
Travel Tickets booking confirmation.
National ID & Passport Copy with proof of residence in home country.
Yellow Fever Medical Certificate
Visa Application Fee (varies depending upon the country applying from, to be paid directly at the High Commission/Embassy of India upon submission of application)

Note : Any additional documents might be required depending on your Nationality, please consult your Edudite counsellor for guidance with application

Step 04: Application for Indian Student Visa
Step 1 - Prepare all documents required for application as given here.
Step 2 - Use a desktop computer or Laptop to go the visa application website, using this link.
Step 3 - Click on the Regular/Paper Visa Application at the bottom left, to open the following window.
Step 4 - Fill your country -> Choose Nearest Indian Mission -> Fill Nationality and other details. Note - Visa Type must be - STUDENT VISA
Note - Purpose must be - FOR HIGHER EDUCATION (INCLUDING IN SOUTH
ASIAN UNIVERSITY AND NALANDA UNIVERSITY), PROVISIONAL STUDENT VISA
Step 5 - Follow the steps and fill out necessary information and upload photo at designated step.
Step 6 - On successful filling of form, note down your application ID and print the form.
Step 7 - Book an appointment for the next available date as per your convenience (well in advance of your travel) from the link provided on the same page.
Step 8 - Visit the selected High Commission of India at least 30 mins before your appointment with all documents and Visa Application fee.
